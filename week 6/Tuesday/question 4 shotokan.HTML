snap punch

A snap punch is a quick, whipping punch that extends fully and retracts quickly. It is often used for speed and precision in striking.

lunge punch

A lunge punch involves stepping forward with one foot while delivering a powerful punch. This technique generates more force by utilizing body weight and momentum.

Hook punch

A hook punch is a curved punch that targets the side of an opponent's body or head. It is delivered with a sweeping motion and is effective for close-range attacks.
